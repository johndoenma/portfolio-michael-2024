Get to know me!

Hi, I'm Michael, a visual and graphic design student based in Honolulu.

I enjoy creatively collaborating with the community to craft visually striking creations tailored to each client's needs.

Outside of design, I love spending my time in the ocean surfing, or on the mats training jiu jitsu. Anything that gets my body moving or allows me to immerse myself in nature is how I enjoy spending my free time.

As a creative, I enjoy extracting inspiration from everyday life. Whether immersed in the city or surrounded by nature, ideas can be found anywhere!
